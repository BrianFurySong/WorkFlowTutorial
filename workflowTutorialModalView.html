Broker

Inviting people to your team.

Introduction

This is the step where you will be making your team. Only subscribers have the option to make a new team.

Step 1

Click the "Team Management" element on the side bar and create a team by clicking the "Create A New Team" button.

Step 2

After you have created a team, find your team in the list of teams under the "Current Teams" headline. Invite your desired team members to the team by using the "+" button.

In Addition

Type the name of the team member in the search bar and it will automatically show a list of members. The "Team Lead" option clarifies the project lead of the team. Click the "Add" button to add and repeat this process until you are satisfied with the members in your team.

Broker

Creating and managing projects.

Introduction

Either the client or the broker is able to create a project. Creating the project depends on whoever has the specific information on the requirements for the desired property. If the client has agreed to create the project, you, as the broker will build the timeline.

Step 1

If you, as the broker, is in charge of creating a project, start with creating a project for your team. Click "Project Overview" on the side bar then find your team by name.

Step 2

Click the "Create Project" button under your team name. Fill in the requirement form then click "Sumbit New Search".

Step 3

Click the "Add Property" button under your team name, then fill in the form with the address and then click the "Add" button. Then fill in the rest of the form with required information then click "Add property".

Step 4

In this step, upload any files pertaining to this property by clicking "Select File" then click "Upload File". (You may upload multiple files) Repeat adding properties by clicking the "Add Another Property" button.

Step 5

Add a timeline by clicking the "Add Timeline" button. (You are able to change the timeline by clicking "Change Template")

Broker

Setting the tour date.

Introduction

As the broker, you will wait until the client sets the tour date.

Step 1

Once the client sets the tour date, a proposed date will appear in your team box. Click the "Edit Tour" button and you have the option to approve the tour date or to change the time. If you decide to change the time, click the "Change Time" button, change the date and time, and approve each property or click the "Approve All" button.

Client

Confirming the tour date.

Introduction

Setting and confirming the tour date may be complicating. Please pay attention to the date as it may change depending on the broker's availability.

Step 1

As the client, you are able to suggest the tour date by clicking the "Create Tour Date" button. In the next screen, click the "Pick Tour Date" button on the lower left corner of the page. Set the date, start time and end time then click the "Save" button. You will have to wait until the broker checks the time.

Step 2

In the box where your team is set, click the "Edit Tour" button and in the next screen, you now will have the option to comfirm the tour date. If you wish to change the time again, then click the "Change Time" button, if you wish to confirm, then click the "Confirm" button.

Step 3

After going on the tour of your properties, you may write down notes about the property but clicking the "Tour Notes" button where the "Confirm" button was in your tour date page. After clicking the "Tour Notes" button, click the "Add Notes" button on the lower right corner of the page then write in your desired message in the text area and click the "Add Note" button. You may even upload a file by clicking the "Select File" button and uploading a file.

Broker

Sending the proposal.

Introduction

After touring the properties, the client will request proposals for the properties they are interested in. When the client requests a proposal, a link "Proposals" will appear next to the 'Project Status' tag.

Step 1

Click the "Proposals" link and click the "Create Lease Proposal" button to fill out the proposal form and upload a proposal then click "Create Lease Proposal".

Step 2

If the client rejects the proposal, it will let you know that it was rejected in the "Edit Lease Proposal" button at the bottom of the property. Edit the proposal and upload a new proposal then click the "Update Lease Proposal" button.

Step 3

If the client accepts the proposal, you will need to upload a counter from the landlord. Do this by first uploading a landlord proposal by clicking the "Select File" button. After the file is uploaded, click the "Upload Counter" button

Step 4

If the client rejects the landlord proposal, the previous steps are repeated until the proposal is approved by the client. When the client approves the proposal from the landlord, you will see a "Lease Drafts" link at the bottom of the list next to the 'Project Status' tag. Click the "Lease Drafts" link and then upload the lease draft by clicking "Select File", upload the lease then click the "Upload Lease Draft" button.

Client

Requesting the proposal.

Introduction

After the touring the properties, you will be able to request a propsal from the desired property.

Step 1

Click on the "Proposal" link next to the 'Project Status' tag (this link will only appear after the tour date has passed).

Step 2

In this screen choose the property or properties that you would like the broker to draft a proposal by clicking the "Request Proposal" at the bottom of each property.

Step 3

After the broker uploads the proposal, go back into the "Properties" page by clicking on the "Proposal" link next to the 'Project Status' tag. Click "View Proposals" at the bottom of each property and then you may either "Approve" or "Reject and Add Note".

Step 4

If you decide to reject the proposal, it will allow you to write a note to the broker about your requests.

Step 5

If you want to accept the proposal, click the "Approve" button and wait for the landlord counter proposal from the broker.

Step 6

Look at the proposal and using the drop down menu on the properties page, approve or reject by selecting either "Yes" or "No". If you decide to reject the proposal, these steps are repeated until you approve the landlord proposal.

Step 7

If you approve the proposal by selecting "Yes" on the drop down menu, the "Submit Signed Documents" button will appear. Click the "Submit Signed Documents" button to end your long journey.
